Louis Thayer Hills

Louis Thayer Hills (1936–2019) was a prolific Wisconsin artist whose work spanned more than six decades. Beginning in the 1950s, he produced a substantial and wide-ranging body of drawings, sketches, sculptures, paintings, and prints. This site honors his artistic legacy and shares information about an upcoming memorial exhibition and art sale, offering collectors the rare chance to acquire original works directly from the estate, with pricing structured to place the work into many hands.
